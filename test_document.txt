Quooker Installation Guide

The Quooker water heater is an innovative kitchen appliance that provides instant boiling water. This installation guide will help you set up your new Quooker system.

Key Features:
- Instant boiling water from the tap
- Energy-efficient water heating system
- Easy to use and maintain
- Professional installation recommended

Installation Steps:
1. Connect the water heater to your main water supply
2. Install the Quooker tap on your kitchen sink
3. Test the system for proper operation
4. The installation is now complete

Safety Notes:
- Always follow safety procedures during installation
- The water heater requires professional maintenance
- Contact Quooker support for any issues

This innovative solution will transform your kitchen experience with instant access to boiling water.
